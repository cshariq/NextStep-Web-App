Welcome to NextStep!

NextStep is an AI-powered mock interview platform designed to help individuals of all backgrounds and abilities excel in job interviews. By offering realistic simulations, personalized feedback, and adaptive interfaces, NextStep supports users with disabilities and varying education levels, providing them with the tools and confidence they need to succeed in real-world job applications.

Join us in preparing for a brighter future with confidence and skill. Learn more about how NextStep can help you succeed in your job search.

Our Mission

Our mission at NextStep is to empower individuals of all backgrounds and abilities by providing a comprehensive, AI-driven mock interview platform. We aim to enhance job readiness and confidence through tailored feedback, adaptive interfaces, and accessible educational resources, ensuring that everyone, regardless of their challenges or education level, has the opportunity to succeed in their career pursuits.

Some of Our Features

Voice Interaction

Allow users to interact with the AI using voice commands and receive responses via text-to-speech.

Diverse Scenarios

Incorporate a variety of mock interview scenarios to cover different roles, industries, and complexity levels.

Educational Resources

Provide links to relevant educational materials, such as interview tips and resume building guides.

Feedback and Metrics

Offer overall metrics on interview performance, including a summary of responses, suggestions for improvement, and a score out of 100.

Adjustable Text

Implement customizable text size and color schemes for users with visual impairments.
